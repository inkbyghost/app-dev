> Haikyu!! (ハイキュー!!, Haikyū!!, from the kanji 排球 "volleyball") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. It was serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from February 2012 to July 2020, with its chapters collected in 45 tankōbon volumes. The story follows Shoyo Hinata, a boy determined to become a great volleyball player despite his small stature.

#### Plot:
Haikyuu!! follows the story of Shoyo Hinata, a determined high school student who dreams of becoming a volleyball star, despite his short stature. After being inspired by a powerful player known as "The Little Giant," Hinata joins his high school’s volleyball team, where he faces a major setback when he’s placed on the same team as his rival, Tobio Kageyama, a talented and sometimes arrogant setter. Despite their initial friction, the two develop a strong partnership, overcoming their differences to become a dynamic duo on the court. The series follows their growth as athletes, building relationships with teammates, and competing in high-stakes tournaments, all while exploring themes of perseverance, teamwork, and self-improvement.
